CyberSpider Game

The project is a single-player, arcade-style, physics-based video game. It puts the player in control of a ball atop a platform. The players goal is to collect all the coins on the starting platform to ascend to the next. The platform tilts as the ball rolls around on it, so the player needs to ensure that the ball doesnâ€™t roll off the platform. For the player to beat the game, they need to collect all the coins on the final platform.

To access and view the code, proceed to the games repository on my GitHub profile: https://github.com/GameDevTeam6/CyberSpider

About this project

This was my BSc Final Project. We had numerous template projects to choose from, and I chose the Unity 3D game. The criteria for the game were that it resembled that of a 80s/90s arcade game, being challenging but compelling, and encourage repetitive for mastery.
